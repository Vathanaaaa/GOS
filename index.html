List the tasks you completed vs what you planned. Be honest — no judgement here.

Root cause only. Not excuses — the real reason. Same for wins: what specifically made it work?

One specific, actionable change. Not "try harder" — a concrete system or behaviour change.

Identify it clearly so you can repeat it. Even a small win counts.
5. What is my single most important mission tomorrow?
One mission. The one that — if completed — makes tomorrow a win regardless of everything else.

Look at your Vision page proximate targets. Are you moving toward them? Any course correction needed?
